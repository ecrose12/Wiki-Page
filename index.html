About

Elizabeth Crose is currently working as an Administrative Assistant in the Business Services Department for Owasso Public Schools. She is also a Techlahoma OKCoders student and Vice President of a local non-profit.

Personal Life

Elizabeth was born and raised in the Tulsa, Oklahoma area. She married William "Billy" Crose in March of 2012 and welcomed two daughters in 2013 and 2016. The Crose Family resides on a 400 acre family farm a few miles south of Oologah, OK where Elizabeth raises chickens, cattle, a donkey, and her pets. Elizabeth enjoys spending time with her family on the farm, traveling, and pursuing her interests in technology, advocacy, and community service.

Volunteer Work

Elizabeth is involved in various volunteer activities within her community and supports local organizations that focus on education and youth development.
